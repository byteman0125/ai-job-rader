🔍 AI Job Radar

🚀 What is AI Job Radar?

AI Job Radar is a powerful browser extension that automatically detects job postings and extracts key information using advanced AI technology.

✨ Key Features

  • AI-Powered Detection: Automatically identifies job postings on any website
  • Smart Extraction: Extracts job title, company, industry, job type, and required skills
  • Keyword Highlighting: Highlights important keywords across the page
  • URL Management: Open multiple job postings simultaneously
  • Real-time Analysis: Continuously monitors page content for updates

🔧 How It Works

  1. Browse to any job posting or company career page
  2. AI Job Radar automatically detects the page type
  3. AI analyzes the full content to extract job information
  4. View extracted data in the floating badge
  5. Use keyword highlighting to focus on important terms

💡 Tips for Best Results

  • Ensure the page is fully loaded before extraction
  • Use the toggle to enable/disable AI extraction as needed
  • Customize keywords for your specific job search
  • Keep your OpenAI API key updated for AI features
